Contact TSA Tracker

Questions, feedback, bug reports, or anything else — we'd love to hear from you.

General Inquiries

For questions about the Service, feedback on wait time accuracy, or general comments, email us at benbirkhahn10@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

Reporting Inaccurate Data

If you notice that a wait time displayed on TSA Tracker looks wrong — for example, it's showing a very long wait during a time the airport was actually quiet, or the data appears to be stuck — please let us know. Include the airport code, the time you noticed the issue, and what you actually observed. This helps us improve data reliability and catch upstream feed problems.

Airport Data Requests

We're always looking to add more airports. If you know of an airport that publishes live TSA wait time data publicly (or you work at an airport and would like to explore a data partnership), please reach out. Include the airport code and any relevant links to data sources you're aware of. Our engineering team reviews every airport request.

Advertising and Sponsorships

TSA Tracker reaches travelers who are actively planning airport trips — a high-intent, travel-focused audience. If you're interested in advertising or a sponsored placement, contact us at the email above with the subject line "Advertising." We review all inquiries.

Privacy and Data Requests

For questions or requests related to our privacy practices or your personal data, please review our Privacy Policy first. If you still have questions, email us with the subject line "Privacy Request."

Press and Media

Journalists or media organizations with questions about TSA Tracker, our data methodology, or airport security wait times in general are welcome to reach out via email. We're happy to provide data, context, or background for stories related to airport travel.
