TSA Tracker shows live TSA security checkpoint wait times for major US airports. Data comes directly from official airport security feeds and refreshes approximately every 2 minutes — no guessing, no stale numbers.

How it works

  • We connect directly to each airport's public security data feed.
  • Wait times update every ~2 minutes and are displayed by checkpoint.
  • Color coding makes it instant: green = short, yellow = moderate, red = long.
  • The trend chart shows the last 12 hours so you can pick the best time to arrive.
